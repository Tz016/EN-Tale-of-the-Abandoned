We are The Catchers Curation, a team of student curators who collect abandoned artwork in New York.

In the quiz you've seen many of the abandoned works that our student curators collect offline in New York, which to many viewers are just as moving as the high-priced paintings on the market,

From November 4th to November 19th, we are organizing an offline flash art exhibition "Tale of the Abandoned: Salvaged Works from Art School Waste" in New York City,
where we will revitalize these abandoned works in a poetic exhibition space and revisit their twisted and jagged "discarded" pasts.
